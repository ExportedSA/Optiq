## üéØ Pricing Strategy Rationale

### **Why This Pricing Works:**

1. **Value-Based Pricing**
   - Priced based on customer ROI, not arbitrary costs
   - If Optiq saves 3-5% on ad spend, it pays for itself 5-10x

2. **Competitive Positioning**
   - 30-50% cheaper than Hyros
   - Similar to SegMetrics but more generous limits
   - Premium enough to signal quality

3. **Self-Serve Model**
   - No sales team overhead = lower prices
   - Faster time to value for customers
   - Transparent pricing builds trust

4. **Land & Expand**
   - Free tier for acquisition
   - Natural upgrade path as businesses grow
   - Annual discounts encourage commitment

5. **Event-Based Limits**
   - Aligns with customer value (more events = more ad spend)
   - Scalable pricing model
   - Fair usage across customer sizes

## üöÄ Go-to-Market Pricing Tactics

### **Launch Strategy:**

1. **Lifetime Deal (Limited Time)**
   - First 100 customers: $99/mo locked-in forever (Starter tier)
   - Creates urgency and early adopters
   - Builds case studies

2. **Annual Discount**
   - 17% off annual plans (2 months free)
   - Improves cash flow and retention

3. **Free Trial**
   - 14-day trial on all paid plans
   - No credit card required
   - Reduces friction

4. **Money-Back Guarantee**
   - 30-day refund policy
   - Reduces perceived risk

### **Upsell Triggers:**

- **Free ‚Üí Starter:** Hit 5K events or need 2nd connector
- **Starter ‚Üí Growth:** Hit 50K events or need 4+ connectors
- **Growth ‚Üí Enterprise:** Need SSO, white-label, or 1M+ events

### **Retention Tactics:**

- **Annual lock-in:** 17% discount
- **Usage alerts:** Notify before overages
- **Success metrics:** Show ROI in dashboard
- **Dedicated support:** On Enterprise tier
